

On you local machine, download Isaac Sim WebRTC Streaming Client from here: 
- https://docs.isaacsim.omniverse.nvidia.com/5.0.0/installation/download.html 

On your local machine terminal, run:
ssh abut37yx@airtower -L 8011:localhost:8011
ssh abut37yx@airtower -L 49100:localhost:49100

within Docker on airtower for isaacsim:
OMNI_KIT_ALLOW_ROOT=1 ./runheadless.sh --/app/livestream/publicEndpointAddress=172.29.5.11 --/app/livestream/port=49100

within Docker on airtower for RoboLab:
python examples/demo/run_env.py  \
    --livestream 2 \
    --kit_args "--/app/livestream/publicEndpointAddress=172.29.5.11 --/app/livestream/port=49100"
